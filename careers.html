Why PTA?

Competitive Wages & Excellent Benefits

We value the well-being of our employees. PTA offers competitive wages, a variety of healthcare insurance options, life insurance, retirement benefits, and more.

Continuing Education

Embodying our Core Value of Growth, education is a high priority. We offer ongoing training and mentorship through our Co-Operative Learning Program, Lunch & Learns, and external training courses.

Progressive Schedule

Our office operates Monday–Thursday 7am–5pm and Friday 7am–12pm. Our flextime plan lets you adjust your schedule to fit your life — not the other way around.

Company Culture

Our Core Values are at the root of all we do. We volunteer in the community, host company events, and yes — daily ping pong breaks are a real thing. You'll genuinely enjoy coming to work.
